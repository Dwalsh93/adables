About Me

Hi there! I'm Dillon, a middle school language arts teacher with a passion for technology. I unkowingly began my coding career in 2007 when I learned HTML through designing blogging platforms for myself to spend time on, not knowing almost a decade and a half later I would be making an official career transition into coding. As an educator I can see the necessity for technology in our every day life, and in 2021 knowing how to use and navigate through your computer is almost a life-skill. I'm looking forward to my career in tech to see how I can bring my love of the computer to others.
